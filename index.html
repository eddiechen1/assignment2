The majority of the countries which hold Michelin stars are concentrated around the USA, UK and China.







Michelin star restaurants appear to be concentrated around coastal cities, as seen in the USA, South America and China.

In conjunction with the choropleth map, it is clear that despite the UK having more Michelin star restaurants than Germany and Austria - Germany and Austria have more restaurants with a higher number of stars.







While the USA leads in all categories of stars, China has more two and three star restaurants than the UK - despite the UK having more stars overall.







It is clear that Modern Cuisine is the most commmon cuisine type, followed by Contemporary. Modern-type and Japanese cuisines dominate the most commonly awarded Michelin star cuisines.
